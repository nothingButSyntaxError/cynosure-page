Cynosure. The Mega Event is Here!

A St. Gregorios High School Event.

We cordially invite you to participate in the first edition of our student-powered inter-school event ‘CYNOSURE’ , to be held on 19th and 20th November, 2022. Through this event, we hope to give the participants an opportunity to be the center of attention at the repertoire of uniquely crafted activities. At St. Gregorios High School, we have always encouraged our students to look beyond the realm of pure academics. Our pool of talented students has come up with an array of ingeniously designed events, ranging from Literary to Fine Arts to Sports, that are bound to appeal to the particular age group for which they have been curated. The logo for Cynosure symbolizes the concept of all eyes being on ‘YOU’ at the event.

The Cynosure Website contains a description of all events along with their rules and regulations. It can also be accessed on our event Instagram page @cynosure.stghs
